# [롤플레이: 가족]
## Q1. [11번 유형: 저녁 초대 일정 협의] I'd like to give you a situation and ask you to act it out. You want to invite another couple to your home for dinner. Ask your family member three or four questions to find out when the best day and time is to have your friends come for dinner.

### ==Answer==
Honey, do you remember Jamie? If you are okay with it, I was thinking of inviting her couple over for dinner. What do you think? Great. So what day works best for you? I heard they're free this Sunday. Would that work for you too? Then what time do you think is good? Is seven okay? I think it's a nice time. Not too early, not too late. And oh yeah, what should we prepare for dinner? If we can't decide right now, I can just ask them what kind of food they like. Alright, that's all set.

## Q2. [12번 유형: 저녁 파티 취소·재조정] I'm sorry, but there's a problem that I need you to resolve. On the day of your dinner party, one of your family members becomes ill. Call your friend and leave a message. Explain what has happened and then propose the plan to have dinner together in the near future.

### ==Answer==
Hi there, Jamie. It's me, Heather. I'm calling about our dinner tonight. I know this is short notice, but I don't think we can make it tonight. My son tested positive for COVID-19 this morning. He's running a high fever and has been coughing a lot. I think it's best for him to rest. So I was hoping we could reschedule our dinner. Would next weekend work for you? Or if you let me know when you are available, we can figure something out. I'll try to be flexible. Okay, give me a call when you get this. Bye.

## Q3. [13번 유형: 가족 때문에 계획 변경 경험] That's the end of the situation. Has anything like that ever happened to you? Have you ever had to change your plans because one of your family members needed your help? Tell me about that episode in detail why you had to change your plans and what happened.

### ==Answer==
*(답변은 Lesson 5 가족 때문에 계획 변경과 동일 유형 → 해당 답변 활용)*

Yes, I had this kind of incident about 10 years ago. My friends and I wanted to take a trip to Jeju Island. We planned everything and booked our flight tickets. However, on the morning of the trip, my older brother and his wife suddenly had a work emergency. They asked me to look after their little kid for the whole day. Since there was nobody else available, I ended up babysitting instead of heading to the airport. I tried to reschedule the trip, but it was not easy. Eventually, I had to cancel everything and I was honestly disappointed. Well, even though I missed that trip, I don't regret helping my brother's family. Things happen and family comes first.
