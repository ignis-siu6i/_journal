# [라이브 음악]
## Q1. [감동의 버스킹 공연] Could you think about a memorable time when you heard live music? Describe that experience in detail. When was it? Where were you? Who were you with? Who did you hear? What happened? What made that performance so memorable or special?

### ==Answer==
Last fall, my family and I went to the park near our home for a small picnic. Nothing fancy. We just grabbed some snacks and headed out. But when we were about to eat on the grass, we saw a huge crowd gathered near the lake. And we could hear music coming from that direction. So of course we got curious and walked over to check it out. And guess what? It was actually a busking event hosted by a TV show and the singer was on stage singing live. Her voice was so powerful and unique that we just froze. People around us were sitting, recording videos and quietly enjoying the moment. The whole vibe felt so calm and kind of dreamy. Well, we ended up watching for almost two hours and totally forgot about our picnic. But honestly, I didn't mind at all because it became a very special memory for my family.

# [가족]
## Q1. [가족 때문에 계획 변경] That's the end of the situation. Has anything like that ever happened to you? Have you ever had to change your plans because one of your family members needed your help? Tell me about that episode in detail why you had to change your plans and what happened.

### ==Answer==
Yes, I had this kind of incident about 10 years ago. My friends and I wanted to take a trip to Jeju Island. We planned everything and booked our flight tickets. So at that point, we were really looking forward to the trip. However, on the morning of the trip, my older brother and his wife suddenly had a work emergency. They asked me to look after their little kid for the whole day. Since there was nobody else available, I ended up babysitting instead of heading to the airport. I tried to reschedule the trip, but you know, it was not easy. Eventually, I had to cancel everything and I was honestly disappointed. Well, even though I missed that trip, I don't regret helping my brother's family. Things happen and family comes first.

# [여행 특별 경험]
## Q1. [유명인을 본 사건] You might have interesting or unexpected experiences while traveling. Tell me about one episode you had that was unforgettable when you traveled. When did it happen? Where were you and who were you with? Tell me all the things that happened to make that experience so unforgettable.

### ==Answer==
About two years ago I went to Jeju Island with my husband for a trip. Around lunchtime we walked into a well-known restaurant that people recommended online. But as soon as we sat down the place felt kind of chaotic so I looked around to see what was going on. Then suddenly I felt this bright energy coming from one corner. Oh my gosh, Chow Ngu was sitting right there. You know him right? He's basically the most handsome actor in Korea. My jaw almost hit the table because I had never seen someone that beautiful in real life. I swear my soul left my body for a second. Then I slowly glanced at my husband and yeah let's just say it wasn't his best moment. I love him but compared to Chow Ngu he looked more like a monkey. I felt a little bad but it was hilarious. So this became one of the funniest episodes I've ever had during a trip.
