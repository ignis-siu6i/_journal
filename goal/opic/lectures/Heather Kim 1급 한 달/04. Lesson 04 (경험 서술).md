# [QT1: 영화]
## Q1. [최근 영화 경험] Tell me the last time you went to a movie. What movie did you see? Who did you see the movie with? What did you do before and after the movie?

### ==Answer==
The last time I went to the movies was a month ago. It was during the weekend and my family wanted to do something fun for our free time, so we decided to watch a movie called My Daughter is a Zombie. We went to CGV Movie Complex in town and there were only a few people around even though it was a weekend. It felt quiet and peaceful. As soon as we arrived, the first thing we did was buy popcorn and soft drinks. My son was especially excited to try the new chocolate flavored popcorn. Then we watched the movie which lasted for about two hours. Oh, the movie was mind blowing. The most striking part was the emotional storyline even though it was just a zombie movie. After the movie, we went to a nearby restaurant and talked about it over dinner. We shared our thoughts on how great the story was. Overall, it was such an enjoyable day.

# [QT1(2): 건강 음식]
## Q1. [건강 음식 경험] Now tell me about the last time you actually ate some healthy food. Where did you eat it and who did you eat it with? How did it taste and what good did it do for you? Give me all the details.

### ==Answer==
The last time I had a healthy meal was last month. My son had an upset stomach that day so he didn't want to eat anything too greasy or heavy. My family decided to order something light and healthy for him instead of cooking. We ordered vegetable porridge from a Korean porridge chain. It's a healthy dish made with rice, carrots, zucchini and mushrooms and it's easy to digest. Something people often eat when they're not feel well. It tasted really clean and mild. Didn't have strong spices but it was warm and comforting. The rice was cooked just right. After the meal we felt much more relaxed and less bloated. My son also enjoyed it and we all agreed it was the right choice for that day. It was a simple meal but it made us all feel warm and healthy.

# [QT3: 여행]
## Q1. [어릴 적 여행 경험] Tell me about some of the trips you took in your youth. Where did you go? Who did you go with and what did you see or do during those early trips?

### ==Answer==
I remember going hiking with my family quite often when I was a kid. My father often suggested hiking trips on weekends and my sister and I always got excited. We used to go to Seoraksan Mountain, one of the most famous mountains in Korea. During the day we would hike along the trails enjoying the fresh air and beautiful nature. The mountain was covered with tall trees and I could hear birds singing. The paths were sometimes steep but the views from the top were amazing and worth the effort. After hiking we often stopped at a small restaurant near the entrance to eat hot and delicious chicken soup. The food always tasted even better after a long hike. Looking back those hiking trips were definitely highlights of my childhood.
