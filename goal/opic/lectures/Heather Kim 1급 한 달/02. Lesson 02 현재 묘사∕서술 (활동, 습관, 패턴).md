## Useful Expressions
- check the showtimes online
- discuss the movie
- grab a quick bite/have a light meal
- go out for dinner or dessert
- get some snacks at the counter
- head straight home if I’m tired
- arrive early to get good seats
- post a short review online
- watch trailers while waiting
- clear the seats / take out trash
- go to the restroom
- take a photo in front of the poster

## Sample Anser
### 1) **도입: I usually make plans with my family to catch a movie together at least once a month.**
### 2) **영화 보기 전: Before watching a movice,** we arrange the details, like when and where to go, and which movie to watch. If needed, we also book our tickets in advance to avoid any hassle.
### 3) **극장에서 하는 일: Well, once we get to the theater,** ==we grab some snacks like popcorn and drinks. After that, we head to the restroom to make sure we’re all set to enjoy the movie without interruptions.==
### 4) **영화 본 후 하는 일: After the movie,** ==we often go for dinner and chat about it. We love discussing the storyline, characters, memorable scenes and so on.==
### 5) **예외적 상황: Sometimes, ==if we feel too tired, we just head straight home instead.==
